# Introduction

If you are building a smart contract wallet, Pimlico allows you to get the UserOperations from your users and sponsor the gas costs in just one API call, for free with no upfront or recurring costs(strings attached). In addition, we give you a part of the revenue we make, providing a valuable revenue stream on top of the UX benefits to users.

If you are interested in receiving an API key and having us help you integrate Pimlico into your smart contract wallet, please fill out our [interest form](https://u2fnk0lj4et.typeform.com/to/ZxnyuGXv) or reach out to us at contact@pimlico.io. We aim to respond as soon as possible, usually in a couple hours.
